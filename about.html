About Us

DraftDeck Fantasy is a National Football League draft website that aims to combine the addicting elements of Fantasy football with the exciting aspect of trading cards.

We are a small group of students with a similar passion for sports. We have always been interested in fantasy sports and trading cards and it is our goal to be able to combine the two to create one product.
